## Limits

- **No persistent connection required.** Fire the create call from a triggered
  worker and respond to the webhook from another. Both can be short-lived.
- **Volume during the pilot** (single digits per day, ramping after the August
  launch) is well within limits. We will size rate limits with you as you scale.
- **Rate limiting** returns [`429`](/api/errors) with a `Retry-After` header.

## Pilot pricing

First month free, then a flat monthly fee up to a transaction threshold, with
volume pricing beyond that, per your agreement with Lane.

## Pilot checklist

<Steps>
  <Step title="Receive your credentials">
    API key, webhook secret, and the one-time [setup](/api/setup) link from Lane.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete setup">
    Create your account at
    [wallet.getonlane.com/home](https://wallet.getonlane.com/home) and add the
    card every order will be charged to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Stand up the webhook receiver">
    And [verify signatures](/api/webhooks#verifying-signatures).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Place a live test order">
    To a real address, from a container or test agent.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Working session">
    Run real purchases together with the Lane team.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Questions during integration go straight to the Lane team.
