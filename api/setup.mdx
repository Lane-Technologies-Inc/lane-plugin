title: One-time setup
description: "Done once, not per order: create your account, add a card, and get your API key and webhook secret."
---

Before your first call, complete a short setup in the Lane wallet. This happens
**once** — not per order.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up at [wallet.getonlane.com/home](https://wallet.getonlane.com/home).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a card">
    This is the card every order will be charged to. Card data goes directly to
    our payment vault and never touches your servers or ours in raw form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get your API key and webhook secret">
    The key authenticates every request. The secret lets you
    [verify webhook signatures](/api/webhooks#verifying-signatures).
  </Step>
</Steps>

After setup, every order is a single headless call. There is no per-order
approval step and nothing for an end user to tap or confirm. Per-order spend is
bounded by the [`max_price`](/api/create-order) you send on each request.

<Warning>
  Your API key spends from one wallet (the card you enrolled). Treat it like a
  secret: anyone holding it can place orders against that wallet.
</Warning>

## Pilot checklist

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account & add a card">
    Sign up at [wallet.getonlane.com/home](https://wallet.getonlane.com/home) and
    enroll the card every order will be charged to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get your API key and webhook secret">
    From the wallet — the key authenticates requests, the secret verifies webhooks.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Stand up your webhook receiver">
    And [verify signatures](/api/webhooks#verifying-signatures).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Place a live test order">
    To a real address, from a container or test agent.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Working session">
    Run real purchases together with the Lane team.
  </Step>
</Steps>
